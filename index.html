About Us

The name 'Espoir' is based from the french word meaning "hope". As a result of the word "hope," we will give good service to our customers so that they are happy when they taste all of the cuisine that we create. We are a cafe that serves luxurious meals, beverages, and desserts at a reasonable price. When it comes to sweets, we provide two options: western desserts and local desserts. So, given our menu's variety, we are convinced that you will like the items supplied. This café is located at Level 11 Naza, Tower, Platinum Park, No 10, Persiaran KLCC, Kuala Lumpur, 50088. We are open every day except Friday from 8:00 A.M. to 10:00 P.M. On Saturday, we open at 10:00 A.M.- 8:00 P.M.

At Espoir Cafe, we uphold our vision and mission in giving the very best, unique and finest quality pastries, cakes and confectionaries. All using only the finest, freshest and best ingredients in our bakeries. We ensure that you’ll enjoy our bakeries until its last crumb
